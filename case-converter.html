Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I convert text to proper case (Capitalize Each Word)?
 To quickly change between lowercase, UPPERCASE, and Capitalize Each Word, select the text and press Shift + F3 (in applications like Microsoft Word). In our Case Converter tool, simply choose Title Case to capitalize the first letter of each word.

What does it mean to "change text to uppercase"?
 Changing text to uppercase means converting all letters in the selected text to capital letters (e.g., "example" becomes "EXAMPLE").

How do I convert uppercase text to lowercase?
 Select the Lowercase option in the Case Converter. This changes all characters in your text to small letters (e.g., "HELLO" becomes "hello").

What is Sentence Case in the Case Converter?
 Sentence case capitalizes only the first letter of the first word in a sentence, just like how you'd write a normal sentence.
