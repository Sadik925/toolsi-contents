Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I crop an image using this tool?

Click the upload button, select your image, drag to adjust the cropping area, then click the download button to save your cropped image.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, the Image Cropper is completely free with no hidden costs or premium features.

What image formats are supported?

You can upload and crop JPG, PNG, or WEBP images. Output format will match the original image type.

Will my images be stored or shared?

No. All cropping is done in your browser, and no image data is uploaded or stored on our servers.
