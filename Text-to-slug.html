Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is a slug in web development?

A slug is a user-friendly part of a URL that typically represents the title or name of a page in lowercase with hyphens instead of spaces.

Why should I use a slug instead of regular text in URLs?

Slugs improve readability, SEO, and consistency in URLs by eliminating spaces, special characters, and capital letters.

Can I customize the output slug?

Yes. You can manually tweak the output after it's generated if you need to adjust certain words, symbols, or formatting.
